M3a Hamoud

Hamoud Boualem is a well-known Algerian company that produces soft drinks and beverages, particularly famous for its flagship product, the "Selecto" soda. Founded in 1878 by Youssef Hammoud in the region of Boualem, Algeria, the company has a long history and has become a household name in Algeria and other North African countries. Hamoud Boualem is recognized for its traditional recipes and quality ingredients, maintaining a strong presence in the market for over a century.
